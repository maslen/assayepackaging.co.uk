Glee - the home of international garden, pet and leisure  16 – 18 September 2007 NEC Birmingham

Glee is the largest annual trade event for the garden, pet and leisure industry. Each September, over 25,000 trade visitors meet at Glee over three days, making it the industry's favourite place to do business.

The diverse selection of products make Glee unique, setting it apart from any other industry event. Visitors can keep up to date with new product launches and retail ideas and catch up with contacts within the industry.

Glee has undergone significant remodelling for the 2007 show, bringing buyers a more logical layout and a more rewarding buying experience. The three core sectors Garden, Pet and Leisure mean complimentary products are grouped more closely together, so buyers can compare and source more of the latest ranges and new launches for the coming year.

Due to its continuing growth, visitors to the show this year will find Petindex in its new home in Halls 6-8. Complete with more facilities and features in this section, you will be able to source all your pet and aquatic needs in one go. Petindex is where you’ll find the biggest suppliers, best brands, latest lines and traditional favourites in the fields of petcare, petfood and pet accessories.

Assaye Packaging Ltd had an excellent show at Glee 2006, meeting many new potential customers in both the horticultural, pet food and pet care worlds. We are looking forward to seeing you in September at Glee 2007.
