Polythene Bags

Polythene Bags (or metallocene, which is the official technical name for the material) are an in-expensive method of packaging dry goods.

Metallocene has high strength and high clarity and can be heat sealed as well as stitched.

Pet food, horse feed, bedding, cat litter, horticultural products and industrial products and powders are regularly packaged in polythene bags.

The finished product is a single structure (as opposed to a laminate).

The printing is Flexographic, printed on the outside.

The metallocene is extruded in the form of a tube, printed, made into a bag and then cut to length in a continuous operation.
