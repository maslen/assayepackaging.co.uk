Shrink Sleeves

The shrink sleeves are produced with printed plastic tubular film that is to be applied to the container and subjected to hot retraction through a specific retraction tunnel by hot air or steam.

The sleeve fits perfectly the required form and, if correctly applied, there is no trace of its presence, giving the impression of having a direct declaration on the container.

The sleeve can cover entirely or partially the container and be used also as warrently seal.

Thanks to special inks and coatings we can offer a wide range of high quality graphic solutions.
