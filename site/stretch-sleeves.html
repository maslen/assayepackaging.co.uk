Stretch Sleeves

These labels made in polyethylene take advantage of the material flexible memory and feature a cold application. The high quality flexography printing as well as the use of extremely transparent and bright materials allow to obtain high level decorations.

The stretch sleeves can be also used as collection strip for several bottles, adding a device as a handle in order to have DECOPAK or MULTIPACK packaging.
